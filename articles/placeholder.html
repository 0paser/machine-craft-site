Getting Started with Tekkit Classic

Published: Feb 2026 • Category: Player Guides

Welcome to the industrial revolution. Tekkit Classic (Minecraft 1.2.5) is a different beast compared to modern modpacks. This guide will help you survive your first night and start your journey toward ProjectE and high-voltage power.

1. The First Tool: The Divining Rod

Before you build a massive factory, you need resources. Unlike modern Minecraft where you just strip mine, Tekkit Classic gives you the Divining Rod. This tool allows you to see the "value" of the blocks in front of you, helping you locate veins of Diamonds and Uranium much faster.

Pro-Tip: Don't throw away your Cobblestone! In this pack, almost everything has an EMC value. You can eventually use an Energy Condenser to turn that "trash" stone into valuable Red Matter.

2. Powering Your Machines

You have two main paths for power: IC2 (IndustrialCraft 2) and BuildCraft. They do not use the same energy!

  • IC2: Uses EU (Energy Units). Think Copper Cables and Macerators.
  • BuildCraft: Uses MJ (Mechanical Joint). Think Stirling Engines and Quarrys.

3. Essential Starting Crafting

Your first goals should be to craft these three items as soon as possible:

  • Macerator: This doubles your ore output. One Iron Ore becomes two Iron Dust.
  • Generator: To power that Macerator. Start with a basic coal-burning one.
  • Transmutation Tablet: The "God-Tier" item of the pack. This allows you to trade items for their EMC value.
