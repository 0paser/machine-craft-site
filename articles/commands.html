Server Commands

How do I protect my Base, Containers, Machines, etc...
  • To claim an area of land, place a Chest for a pre-defined area or use a Golden Shovel for a custom area. Claim blocks are accrued for every hour of logon time.
  • /abandonclaim - Delete the claim you're standing in.
  • /abandonallclaims - Delete ALL claims you own.
  • /trust - Grant another Player FULL access to your claim(s).
  • /untrust - Remove another Players access to your claim(s).
  • /cprivate - Protect a Container, Machine, or door.
  • /cpublic - Allow the public to access a container, machine, or door while retaining ownereship.
  • /cremove - Remove all protections from a container, machine, or door.
What can I do to get around quickly...
  • /spawn - Teleport to the Spawn.
  • /sethome - Sets the coordinate you are standing on as your "Home".
  • /home - Teleport to your "Home".
  • /delhome - Remove your current "Home".
  • /tpa - Request to teleport to another Player.
  • /tpaccept - Accept a teleport request.
